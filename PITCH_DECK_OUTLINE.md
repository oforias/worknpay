## Presentation Tips

**Timing:** 10-15 minutes presentation + 10 minutes Q&A

**Key Messages:**
1. Huge underserved market (11M workers)
2. Strong unit economics (23:1 LTV:CAC)
3. Clear path to profitability (Month 18)
4. Experienced team with local knowledge
5. First-mover advantage in growing market

**Anticipate Questions:**
- How will you acquire workers at scale?
- What prevents competitors from copying you?
- Why now? Why Ghana?
- What are the regulatory risks?
- How do you ensure quality control?
- What's your customer acquisition strategy?
- How will you handle disputes?

**Delivery:**
- Be passionate but data-driven
- Tell stories (worker/customer examples)
- Show confidence in numbers
- Address risks proactively
- End with clear ask and next steps
