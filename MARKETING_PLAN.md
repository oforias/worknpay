# WorkNPay Marketing Plan

## Marketing Overview

### Objectives
**Year 1:**
- Acquire 5,000 verified workers
- Acquire 25,000 active customers
- Achieve 50,000 completed bookings
- Build brand awareness in Greater Accra
- Establish WorkNPay as the trusted service marketplace

**Year 2:**
- Grow to 15,000 workers
- Expand to 100,000 customers
- Geographic expansion (Kumasi, Takoradi)
- Achieve 200,000 completed bookings

### Budget
**Year 1 Total: GH₵ 400,000**
- Digital Marketing: 40% (GH₵ 160,000)
- On-Ground Activation: 25% (GH₵ 100,000)
- Content & PR: 15% (GH₵ 60,000)
- Referral Programs: 15% (GH₵ 60,000)
- Events & Partnerships: 5% (GH₵ 20,000)

## Target Audience Analysis

### Customer Personas

**Persona 1: Busy Professional "Ama"**
- Age: 28-40
- Occupation: Corporate employee, entrepreneur
- Income: GH₵ 3,000-8,000/month
- Location: East Legon, Airport Residential, Cantonments
- Pain Points: No time to search for workers, trust issues, payment hassles
- Motivations: Convenience, reliability, quality assurance
- Digital Behavior: Active on Instagram, Facebook, uses mobile money daily
- **Acquisition Channel:** Facebook/Instagram ads, Google search

**Persona 2: Homeowner "Kwame"**
- Age: 35-55
- Occupation: Business owner, senior professional
- Income: GH₵ 5,000-15,000/month
- Location: Spintex, Tema, Achimota
- Pain Points: Finding reliable workers for home maintenance
- Motivations: Property maintenance, quality work, accountability
- Digital Behavior: Uses WhatsApp, searches Google for services
- **Acquisition Channel:** Google Ads, referrals, corporate partnerships

**Persona 3: Diaspora/Expat "Sarah"**
- Age: 25-50
- Occupation: Works abroad or expatriate in Ghana
- Income: High disposable income
- Location: Airport Residential, Labone, Osu
- Pain Points: Can't verify workers, need services for family/property
- Motivations: Trust, transparency, digital payments
- Digital Behavior: Heavy social media user, prefers apps
- **Acquisition Channel:** Facebook groups, expat communities, PR

## Marketing Strategies

### 1. Worker Acquisition Strategy

#### A. Direct Outreach (Budget: GH₵ 60,000)
**Tactics:**
- Field agents visit repair shops, markets, trade centers
- On-site registration assistance
- Immediate verification process
- Welcome bonus: First 3 bookings commission-free

**Locations:**
- Accra Central (phone repair shops)
- Madina Market (electronics)
- Kaneshie Market (various trades)
- Spintex (auto electricians)
- Circle (gadget repair)

**KPIs:**
- 100 workers/month recruited
- 70% activation rate (complete profile)
- 50% completion rate (first booking)

#### B. Trade Association Partnerships (Budget: GH₵ 20,000)
**Target Associations:**
- Ghana Electrical Contractors Association
- Ghana Plumbers Association
- Ghana Hairdressers and Beauticians Association
- National Association of Graduate Teachers

**Approach:**
- Presentation at association meetings
- Bulk registration drives
- Special commission rates for members
- Co-branded marketing materials

#### C. Vocational School Partnerships (Budget: GH₵ 15,000)
**Target Schools:**
- Accra Technical Training Centre
- Don Bosco Technical Institute
- Other TVET institutions

**Programs:**
- Career day presentations
- Graduate onboarding program
- Skills verification partnerships
- Internship opportunities

#### D. Referral Program (Budget: GH₵ 30,000)
**Structure:**
- Worker refers another worker: GH₵ 20 bonus
- Bonus paid after referee completes 3 bookings
- Unlimited referrals
- Leaderboard with monthly prizes

**Promotion:**
- In-app notifications
- WhatsApp broadcasts
- Success stories

### 2. Customer Acquisition Strategy

#### A. Digital Advertising (Budget: GH₵ 160,000)

**Google Ads (GH₵ 80,000)**
- **Search Campaigns:**
  - Keywords: "plumber near me", "phone repair Accra", "electrician Ghana"
  - "laptop repair", "home tutor Accra", "AC repair"
  - Budget: GH₵ 5,000/month
  - Target CPC: GH₵ 2-4
  - Expected clicks: 1,250-2,500/month

- **Display Network:**
  - Retargeting website visitors
  - Lookalike audiences
  - Budget: GH₵ 2,000/month

**Facebook & Instagram Ads (GH₵ 60,000)**

- **Conversion Campaigns:**

  - Retargeting engaged users

**Ad Creative Themes:**

## Metrics and KPIs

### Acquisition Metrics
- **CAC (Customer):** Target GH₵ 16
- **CAC (Worker):** Target GH₵ 80
- **Conversion Rate:** Website to signup
- **Activation Rate:** Signup to first booking
- **Channel ROI:** Revenue per channel

### Engagement Metrics
- **Bookings per Customer:** Target 2 in Year 1
- **Worker Utilization:** Bookings per worker per month
- **Repeat Rate:** % customers with 2+ bookings
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score):** Target 50+

### Brand Metrics
- **Brand Awareness:** Unaided recall in target market
- **Social Media Followers:** Growth rate
- **Website Traffic:** Monthly visitors
- **App Downloads:** (When launched)

### Financial Metrics
- **GMV:** Gross Merchandise Value
- **Revenue:** Commission + fees
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** Target 20:1+
- **Payback Period:** Target < 3 months

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Digital Adoption
**Mitigation:**
- On-ground support for worker onboarding
- Simple, intuitive UI
- Video tutorials in local languages
- Phone support

### Risk: High CAC
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on organic channels (SEO, referrals)
- Optimize ad campaigns continuously
- Build strong brand for word-of-mouth
- Leverage partnerships

### Risk: Poor Quality Workers
**Mitigation:**
- Strict verification process
- Rating system
- Proactive quality monitoring
- Quick dispute resolution

### Risk: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage
- Build strong brand
- Network effects
- Superior product

## Conclusion

This marketing plan provides a comprehensive roadmap for acquiring workers and customers, building the WorkNPay brand, and achieving Year 1 targets. Success depends on:

1. **Aggressive worker recruitment** through direct outreach
2. **Efficient customer acquisition** via digital channels
3. **Strong brand building** for trust and credibility
4. **Community engagement** for retention and referrals
5. **Continuous optimization** based on data

With disciplined execution and budget management, WorkNPay will establish itself as Ghana's leading service marketplace within 12 months.
